How do I join?
Send us an email with your name, number, and your daughter's name and age. Have your budding Marie Curie write 3-5 sentences about why she would like to join the program. You may type her writing directly into the email or send a photo of her writing written on a sheet of paper. Stay tuned for info about upcoming sessions, and we hope to see you soon!>
*See "Classes" for more information about dates, times, location, and costs.
